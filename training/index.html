Mentorship That Makes a Difference

At Global Edge, mentorship is the foundation of our training model — not an add-on. Every student and professional receives individual attention and dedicated guidance throughout their learning journey.

For Students (Grades 9–12)

  • Dedicated Mentors for personalized academic support
  • Weekly quizzes, mock tests, and doubt-clearing sessions
  • Progress tracking and parent updates
  • Subject specialists in Math, Physics & Chemistry

For Professionals (ISO Courses)

  • Certified Lead Auditors as trainers and mentors
  • Hands-on training with real-life case studies
  • Support in exam preparation, career guidance, and audit readiness
Our mentorship program ensures accountability, clarity, and success, regardless of the learning path you choose.
